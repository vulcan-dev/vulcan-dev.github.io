  • TeardownM Discontinued

    Back in 2021, TDMP was the only multiplayer mod. Soon after, Teardown released an update which broke many offsets and signatures that the mod uses to communicate with the game.

    Not long after, @Nahu created a C# mod loader for Teardown called Sledge. After release, I heard someone called @alexandargyurov was working on a multiplayer mod for Teardown. I was very excited to see this and I decided to help him out. After a few weeks, we got a playable version. We had a dedicated server and we could connect, run around and drive vehicles.

    A few months later, Teardown once again released an update which broke the mod. After a while of trying to fix it, we all lost interest and decided to stop working on it.

    After a long time, I decided to pick it up again and after speaking to @DangerKiddy, he said he's been working on TDMP again so that's when I decided to work on that instead.
